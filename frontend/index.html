About Blood Bridge

Our Mission

Blood Bridge is dedicated to connecting blood donors with patients in need, streamlining the process of blood donation, and ultimately saving lives through efficient and timely blood transfusions.

We believe that access to blood should never be a barrier to life-saving medical care. By leveraging technology, we aim to create a community of donors ready to respond to emergencies and routine needs alike.

How It Works

  1. Register as a Donor

    Create a profile with your blood type, location, and contact information.

  2. Respond to Notifications

    Receive alerts when your blood type is needed in your area.

  3. Coordinate Donation

    Connect with the hospital or blood bank to arrange your donation.

  4. Save Lives

    Your donation can help save up to three lives with each blood donation.

Blood Donation Facts

  • A single car accident victim can require up to 100 units of blood.
  • Someone in the world needs blood every two seconds.
  • One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Less than 10% of eligible people donate blood worldwide.
  • Blood cannot be manufactured; it can only come from donors.
  • The average adult has about 10 pints of blood in their body.
  • The most common blood type is O+, followed by A+.
  • The rarest blood type is AB-, which is found in less than 1% of the population.
